### Welcome to the London hub hosted at Imperial College London

Join us at Imperial College London for the London hub of the 3rd Global LLM Hackathon for Materials Science & Chemistry. Over two days (11–12 Sept), collaborate locally whilst connecting with teams worldwide to prototype AI/LLM solutions for real research challenges.

- **When:** 11–12 September 2024, 09:00–18:00 daily
- **Where:** Room G06, Royal School of Mines, Imperial College London
- **Access:** South Kensington station (5–7 min walk via Exhibition Road)
- **Who's welcome:** Everyone—researchers, students, and the AI‑curious. No prior experience required; bring a laptop and enthusiasm.
- **What we provide:** Workspace, high‑speed internet, all meals, and lots of coffee
