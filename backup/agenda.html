Hackathon Agenda

Pre-Hackathon: July 15 - September 10

Brainstorm, build, team up.

Before the event, participants can join the Slack and Miro and attend the initial teaming event. Pre-teaming and beginning work is allowed!

  1. Add the events to your calendar.
  2. Join the Slack channel to connect with other participants.
  3. Brainstorm project ideas and form teams:
    • Browse the project proposals on our Miro board.
    • Post your project idea in the slack channel with the #team hashtag.
    • If you would like to join a team, use the slack channel to express your interest and describe your background, again using the #team hashtag.
    • Submit your project proposal.

Weekly Talks: August

Leading up to the hackathon, we will be hosting weekly talks from experts in the field. All talks will be streamed live and available on our YouTube channel.

Week 1: From MIT PhD to AI Startup: Creating Better Benchmarks for AI-Driven Scientific

Speaker: Chenru Duan, Founder and CTO of Deep Principle
Topic: Chenru discusses his journey from MIT PhD to AI startup and introduces the groundbreaking Scientific Discovery Evaluation (SDE) Framework which focuses on textbook-style Q&A, SDE Harness evaluates LLMs on iterative scientific discovery workflows - hypothesis generation, experimentation, observation, and refinement.
