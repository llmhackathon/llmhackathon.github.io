How to Submit

  1. Provide a brief documentation of the code implementation, including setup instructions and usage examples (e.g. screenshots, code snippets along with instructions in README.md).
  2. Create a concise video presentation (2 minutes or less) summarizing your teamâ€™s project, highlighting its applications in materials science and chemistry, and showcasing the innovative use of LLMs.
  3. Post your video to at least one of the following social media platforms: YouTube, Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram, or Threads. Ensure that your video is public and easily accessible.
  4. One team member should fill out this google form to submit your project.
  5. !! Submissions must be received by Sep 12nd at 17:00 pm Central US Time (CT) !!
